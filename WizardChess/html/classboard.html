Detailed Description

Board class, deals with the movement of the pieces, gets valid moves, verifies starting position of the chess board.

Does all activities related to the board, board states and moves. Uses functions from thc.cpp to complete chess functions.

Authors
Jarvis, Rustam, Owen, Hassan, Dayton
